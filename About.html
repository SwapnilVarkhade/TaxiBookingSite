The Quickest Way to Your Destination

TAXIWALA is India’s largest mobility platform and one of the world’s largest ride-hailing companies, serving 250+ cities across India, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK.

The TAXIWALA app offers mobility solutions by connecting customers to drivers and a wide range of vehicles across bikes, auto-rickshaws, metered taxis, and cabs, enabling convenience and transparency for hundreds of millions of consumers and over 1.5 million driver-partners.
TAXIWALA’s core mobility offering in India is supplemented by its electric-vehicle arm, Ola Electric; India’s largest fleet management business
TAXIWALA was founded in Dec 2023 by Swapnil Varkhade ,Pavan Mungale and Vishnu Nakulwad with a mission to build mobility for a billion people.
